Gawe.id
Mobile Application (Android, iOS)

Gawe.id is one of the mobile apps on Android and iOS that we have made. An easy to use job portal where high school graduates can meet their future job and company.

PROBLEM

Gawe company already has their job portal at their website, which is gawe.id. Their target is people who has graduated from high school (SMA/SMK). Their segment is blue collar (worker on field). Gawe has role to connect companies that are searching for talents with the job seekers.
