About GITS Indonesia

GITS Indonesia is a company in information technology, especially software development. GITS Indonesia services its client by utilizing information technology to expand the clients' companies and increases their competition value. Our clients or target markets are government instances and companies, regardless of their business scales, which need the use of information technology.

We focuses in developing applications for digital marketing, which are applications that can help clients to make use of the advancement in information technology to increase their marketing value. This digital marketing concept may be implemented in many different medias, such as portal, interactive mobile application for iPhone, Android, Blackberry, J2ME, etc.

Why Choose Us?

We are passionate about IT, especially mobile technology. Our mission is to create great mobile applications that can make our lives more efficient on the go.

  • Providing the best solution for clients' businesses through professional IT system improvement.
  • Owning resource in form of a solid team who are experts in their own fields.
  • Focusing in IT application which helps the marketing system of the clients' products by implementing digital marketing applications.
  • Innovating techniques and idea of digital marketing.
  • Giving satisfaction for clients by providing sustainable services.
